#HEALTH BUDDY PROJECT 

## Ultimate Goal of Your Project

You want to create a premium, AI-powered health monitoring system that covers all dimensions of health:

Physical Health – Track vital signs, provide diet & workout recommendations, analyze blood pressure, sugar levels, BMI (optional).

Mental Health – Monitor stress, sleep, mood; suggest mindfulness exercises, therapy, or awareness content.

Predictive Health – AI/ML-based disease prediction from symptoms.

Preventive & Awareness – Articles, videos, tips to reduce health risks.

Analytics & Reporting – Dashboard and PDF reports summarizing all insights.

Engagement & Gamification – Suggest games, exercises, or activities to improve health.

In short: your website should act like a personal health companion, giving users guidance, predictions, and awareness across all dimensions.

Step 1 – Health Risk Analysis (Done)

Health articles (BP, sugar, heart care, mental health)
